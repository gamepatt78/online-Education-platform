Setting Up Your First Class

Steps to Create Your First Class

  • Go to the Classes section from the sidebar.
  • Click "Create New Class" or "Add Class".
  • Enter the class name, subject, and description.
  • Add students by selecting from the list or uploading a CSV file.
  • Assign subjects and teachers to the class.
  • Set up the class schedule and resources (PDFs, videos, links).
  • Save your class. You can edit details or add more students later.

Tip: Use the Resources section to upload teaching materials for your class.
