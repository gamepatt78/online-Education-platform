Navigation Guide

How to Navigate the Teacher Portal

  • Sidebar: Use the sidebar on the left to access Assignments, Attendance, Questions, Profile, Calendar, Resources, and more.
  • Dashboard: The dashboard gives you a quick overview of upcoming events, assignments, and notifications.
  • Top Bar: Access your profile, settings, and logout options from the top right corner.
  • Search: Use the search bar to quickly find resources, students, or help topics.
  • Notifications: Click the bell icon to view important updates and alerts.
  • Calendar: View and manage your academic schedule and events.
