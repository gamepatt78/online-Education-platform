Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

  • Login Problems: Make sure your username and password are correct. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" link or contact support.
  • Page Not Loading: Check your internet connection and refresh the page. Try clearing your browser cache.
  • File Upload Errors: Ensure your file is within the allowed size and format (PDF, DOCX, etc.). Try renaming the file and uploading again.
  • Missing Notifications: Check your notification settings in your profile. Make sure browser notifications are enabled.
  • Slow Performance: Close unused tabs and applications. Make sure your device meets the system requirements.
  • Other Issues: Restart your browser or device. If the problem persists, contact support for assistance.

If your issue is not listed, please reach out to our support team for personalized help.
