Creating Assignment

How to Create an Assignment

  • Navigate to the Assignments section from the sidebar.
  • Click "Create Assignment" or "Add New Assignment".
  • Fill in the assignment title, description, total marks, and due date.
  • Attach relevant files (PDFs, Docs, images) for students to download.
  • Select the class or group to assign the work to.
  • Set submission instructions and any grading criteria.
  • Click Save or Publish to make the assignment available to students.

Tip: You can edit or update assignments anytime before the due date.
