Providing Feedback

How to Provide Feedback to Students

  • Go to the Assignments or Feedback section in the portal.
  • Select the student or group you want to give feedback to.
  • Type your comments, suggestions, or encouragement in the feedback box.
  • Attach files, links, or resources if needed for further learning.
  • Submit your feedback; students will be notified automatically.
  • Review previous feedback history to track student progress.

Tip: Timely and constructive feedback helps students improve and stay motivated.
