Setting Deadlines

How to Set Assignment Deadlines

  • Go to the Assignments section and click "Create Assignment" or edit an existing one.
  • Find the Due Date field and select the deadline date and time for submission.
  • Communicate the deadline clearly in the assignment description.
  • Students will see the deadline on their dashboard and receive reminders.
  • You can extend or modify deadlines by editing the assignment before the due date.
  • After the deadline passes, submissions may be marked as late or closed, based on your settings.

Tip: Setting clear deadlines helps students manage their time and stay organized.
