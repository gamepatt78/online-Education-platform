Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

  • How do I reset my password?
    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.
  • How do I upload resources?
    Go to the Resources section and click "Upload Resource".
  • Can I edit an assignment after publishing?
    Yes, you can edit assignments until the due date.
  • How do I contact support?
    Visit the Contact Support page for details.
  • How do I export attendance reports?
    Go to Attendance, select your class and date range, then click "Export".
  • Why am I not receiving notifications?
    Check your notification settings in your profile and ensure browser notifications are enabled.
