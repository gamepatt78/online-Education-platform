Proctoring Guidelines

  • Monitor live exam sessions for suspicious activity.
  • Flagged students should be reviewed for possible violations.
  • Remind students to keep their webcam and microphone on during the exam.
  • Use the remarks column to note any interventions or issues.
  • Export student proctoring data for record-keeping and reporting.

Proctoring Actions

  • Send Warning: Instantly notify students about suspicious activity.
  • Live Chat: Communicate with students during the exam for clarifications.
  • Session Recording: Enable/disable video and audio recording for review.
  • Download Reports: Export detailed proctoring logs in PDF or Excel format.
  • Manual Review: Mark students for manual review after the exam.

Proctoring Tips

  • Encourage students to join the exam session 10 minutes early for system checks.
  • Remind students to have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment.
  • Use the chat feature to resolve minor technical issues without interrupting the exam.
  • Regularly update proctoring software for the latest security features.
  • Review flagged sessions soon after the exam for timely resolution.
