Grading Guide

How to Grade Assignments

  • Go to the Assignments section and select the assignment to grade.
  • Open each student's submission to review their work.
  • Enter marks and comments in the grading fields provided.
  • Attach feedback files or notes if needed.
  • Save grades to update student records.
  • Students will be notified of their grades and feedback automatically.

Tip: Use rubrics for consistent and transparent grading.
